Research Interests


      My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. In general, the goal of computer vision is to develop the algorithms and representations that will allow a computer to autonomously analyze large-scale visual/multimedia information. Specifically

  • Large-scale statistical machine learning methods on visual/multimedia data, such as deep neural network learning, multi-task learning, manifold learning, etc.
  • Learn and recognize visual object categories, and scalable deep learning methods for deep neural network-based retrieval and visual search.
  • Practical applications of computer vision and image processing methods to problems in traffic surveillance systems, large-scale retrieval/recognition system.
